WHAT YOU NEED TO KNOW!

WHAT NOT TO DO!

1. When you are finished with the counter

DO NOT press the "X" at the top right

of the screen, as you stats WILL NOT

SAVE. All you need to do is go back

to the MENU and select SAVE AND EXIT.

The window will automatically close

and your stats will be saved.

2. DO NOT tamper with the folders or the

scripts doing so will result the counter

not function properly.

===========================================

WHAT TO DO

1. Place the two .bat files in your desktop

then double click on the "Installer.bat" file

then it will have a loading time, during this

time the .bat file will create the files needed

for the counter to work.

2. When you want to open the counter always

use the "Installer.bat" as it makes sure that

the corret files are where they should be.

3. When you manually putting in the amount

of SAVES and DEATHS. The batch file only

works when you put it in one at a time. Example

If you want to put in 1 saved life you simply

1s

or 1S

Doing so will update the counter with +1 to save.

Putting something else in could result in a crash

and you could loose you stats.

4. The commands for the counter are straight

forward.

Adding to the SAVE total all you need to put in is

1s then pressing ENTER

or

1S then pressing ENTER

Adding to the DEATH total

1d then pressing ENTER

or

1D then pressing ENTER

Pretty easy to get your head around plus

don't put a space between then as the counter

will not update.
