In the season premiere "First Time Again", Rosita tends to Tara before tending to Glenn and Nicholas, and is present for Rick addressing the community of the nearby walker herd endangering them and assigns Rosita, Spencer and Holly to be on guard tower duty. Rosita later helps Abraham build a barricade on one of the main roads to veer the herd away and agrees to help Jessie use a gun. In the episode "JSS", Alexandria is unexpectedly attacked by bandits, called "The Wolves", and Rosita and Aaron take part in the counterattack, killing several Wolves themselves. Spencer approaches Rosita and asks how she and her group were able to keep going with nothing but she tells him that to survive you keep finding something to lose. In the episode "Now", Rosita comforts Maggie when Michonne tells her of Glenn's apparent death but is distracted by Rick running towards the gate with half the herd behind him and barely manages to close the gate after he gets in. Rick addresses the community highlighting that Daryl, Sasha and Abraham succeeded in drawing half the herd away and knows they, Glenn and Nicholas will get back (unaware of the Nicholas' death), but Rosita begins to feel uneasy knowing Abraham is still outside. Rosita later stands guard at the gate, sobbing over Abraham, but stops when Spencer approaches her and offers to take her place which she agrees to, and she commends him for stopping the truck earlier as it would have made their situation much worse if he had not stopped it.
